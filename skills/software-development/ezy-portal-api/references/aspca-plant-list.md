## Toxic Plant Families

### Araceae (Oxalatos de calcio insoluble)
Oral irritation, drooling, vomiting, difficulty swallowing.
- Elephant's Ear (Caladium), Dieffenbachia, Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum),
  **Philodendron (all varieties)**, Pothos (Epipremnum aureum), Chinese Evergreen (Aglaonema),
  Calla Lily (Zantedeschia), Jack-in-the-pulpit, Skunk Cabbage, Arrow-Head Vine (Syngonium),
  Anthurium, **Monstera deliciosa**, Ceriman

### Amaryllidaceae (Lycorine alkaloids)
Vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal pain, tremors, cardiac arrhythmias.
- Daffodil (Narcissus), Amaryllis, Clivia, Onion/Garlic/Chives/Leek (Allium — also causes hemolytic anemia)

### Solanaceae (Solanine alkaloids)
GI upset, CNS depression, respiratory depression.
- Nightshade (Solanum), Deadly Nightshade, Jerusalem Cherry (Solanum pseudocapsicum),
  Tomato leaves/stems, Brunfelsia, Tobacco (Nicotiana)

### Ericaceae
Vomiting, diarrhea, depression, cardiovascular collapse.
- Azalea/Rhododendron, Lily-of-the-Valley Bush (Pieris), Mountain Laurel (Kalmia),
  Fetterbush (Leucothoe), Lambkill (Kalmia augustifolia)

### Liliaceae
Vomiting, depression, diarrhea, tremors.
- Hosta, Daylily (Hemerocallis), Tulip (bulbs most toxic), Hyacinth,
  Asparagus Fern, Gloriosa Lily, Iris

### Crassulaceae (Bufadienolidos)
GI upset, cardiac abnormalities.
- Kalanchoe, **Jade Plant (Crassula)**, Chinese Jade (Crassula arborescens)

### Euphorbiaceae
- Poinsettia (mild), Castor Bean (severe), Pencil Cactus (Euphorbia tirucalli),
  Copperleaf (Acalypha), Croton (Codiaeum)

### Rosaceae (Cyanogenic glycosides in pits/seeds)
Dilated pupils, respiratory distress, brick-red mucous membranes.
- Apple (seeds), Apricot/Peach/Plum/Cherry (pits/stems — cyanide)

## Non-Toxic (Safe for pets)

Basil, Bamboo, Camellia, Cilantro, Dill, Fennel, Hibiscus, Impatiens,
Jasmine, Lavender, Marigold, Sunflower, Orchid, Petunia, Portulaca,
Rosemary, Sage, Snapdragons, Thyme, Zinnia, Boston Fern, Polka Dot Plant,
Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum), Swedish Ivy
